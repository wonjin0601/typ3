FAQ

If you can't find the answer to your question here, get in contact with us via email.

If you are looking for specific guidance on what prompts can be sold on PromptBase, read our prompt submission guidelines.

General

What is PromptBase?

PromptBase is a marketplace for buying and selling prompts for DALL·E, GPT, Stable Diffusion + Midjourney. Prompts are sentences of text that are fed into complex AI models to get a desired output, for example and image of a certain object in a certain style.

What is a Prompt Engineer?

A Prompt Engineer is a new kind of technician, skilled at crafting the text prompts required for an AI model to produce consistent outputs (e.g. images, text or code).

Payments

What is the revenue split?

Prompt sellers keep 80% of every sale of their prompt, and on custom prompt jobs. PromptBase takes a 20% fee which helps run our platform and offer services like support.

How do I get paid?

PromptBase uses Stripe to send out payments to you.

How will I know how many sales I've made?

Within your account page, you'll have access to an internal Stripe dashboard for viewing sales of your prompts.

My country is not appearing when trying to sell my prompt?

We use Stripe as our payments provider for sending payments to our Prompt Sellers. Unfortunately, for certain countries this is not yet supported. We are working hard to find a solution - check out our Twitter page for the latest updates. You can also enter your country on this page to receive notifications when Stripe becomes available

Known countries affected:

  • Argentina
  • Brazil
  • Bangladesh
  • India
  • Mexico
  • Sri Lanka
  • Tunisia
  • Ukraine
How do I start accepting custom prompt payments?

To become eligible to accept custom prompt payments, you must have made 5 sales from submitted prompts on the marketplace.

Prompts

How long does it take for my prompt to be approved?

We aim to review and approve a prompt within 1-3 working days.

Do I still own my prompt if I list it?

Yes. You retain all rights to your prompt, and are welcome to remove it from PromptBase at any point.

How does a prompt get featured?

The featured section is handpicked from some of the best submissions to PromptBase. Generally these prompts demonstrate a unique original idea, and work extremely consistently.

To increase your chances of getting featured, make sure to upload 9 1:1 square images alongside your prompt, as we post featured prompt collages on our Instagram and Twitter pages.

How are thumbnail images selected?

When you upload your prompt, a random thumbnail will be generated for it. However, our review team will alter this thumbnail to select three images that we think will make it the most marketable.

How are trending prompts determined?

Trending prompts are determined by considering sales, views and the time they were uploaded.

How are most popular prompts determined?

Most popular prompts are determined purely on their sales and views.

I can't find my Midjourney profile link?

You'll need an active Midjourney subscription in order to get a verification link to your profile.

Watch our video during the sign up flow in order to locate your profile link.

Profiles

Why are my prompt views/sales/favorites not appearing on my profile?

Profile views, favorites and sales are calculated from across all your prompts. We periodically update this every 12 hours, so there will be a delay in updating the numbers.

What is PromptBase rank?

PromptBase rank is your rank as a seller on the site. It is displayed on your profile if you are within the top 500 sellers. We take into account views, sales and favorites to determine this. Rank #1 is the top seller on the site.

How do I get category skill badges on my profile?

To get emoji badges for a model (like Midjourney) on your profile, you need to get 2 sales using that model.

To get category emoji badges (e.g. 3D, Fantasy, Graphic Design), you need 5 sales in that category. If you have achieved 5 sales in more than 5 categories, we'll only show the top 5 categories you have sales in.
